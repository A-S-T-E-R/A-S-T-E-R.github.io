The Elder Scrolls II: Daggerfall is the second game in the Elder Scrolls series, released in 1996. The story follows the player, sent by the Emperor to free the ghost of King Lysandus from his earthly shackles and uncover the mystery behind a lost letter sent to the former queen of Daggerfall.

The game takes place in the provinces of High Rock and Hammerfell, centered around the Iliac Bay region. As the player, you navigate political intrigue, ancient prophecies, and supernatural forces while interacting with various factions, guilds, and noble houses.

Throughout your journey, you encounter the enigmatic Underking, uncover the secrets of the Numidium—a powerful golem—and ultimately influence the fate of the region through multiple possible endings. Your choices shape the world, making Daggerfall one of the most ambitious RPGs of its time.
