The Elder Scrolls: Arena is the first game in the Elder Scrolls series, released in 1994. The story follows the betrayal of Emperor Uriel Septim VII by his Imperial Battlemage, Jagar Tharn, who imprisons the Emperor in another realm and assumes his identity.

You, the player, take on the role of the Eternal Champion, tasked with rescuing the Emperor. Guided by the spirit of Ria Silmane, Tharn’s former apprentice, you must travel across Tamriel to find the pieces of the Staff of Chaos, the only artifact capable of breaking Tharn’s spell and freeing Uriel Septim VII.

Your journey takes you through various provinces, dungeons, and cities, battling Tharn’s minions and uncovering secrets. Once the Staff is fully restored, you confront Tharn in the Imperial Palace, defeat him, and restore the rightful Emperor to the throne, bringing peace back to Tamriel.
