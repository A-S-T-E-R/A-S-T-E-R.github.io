Cloud 9 Surfing Spot

Cloud 9 is Siargao’s most iconic surf destination, famous for its powerful right-hand barrel waves. Best visited between **September and November**, the reef break produces consistent 6–12 ft barrels thanks to northeast swells. It has hosted the **Cloud 9 Surfing Cup since 2004**, attracting surfers worldwide.

⚠️ Note: Cloud 9 is not for beginners. The reef is shallow (3–6 ft depth) with sharp coral, requiring intermediate to advanced skills. Surfers must also be cautious of strong rips and Siargao’s typhoon season (August–October).

Budget travelers can expect ₱20,000–40,000 per week including flights, with hostel dorms from ₱400/night and board rentals ₱1,000–1,500/day. For spectators, the wooden boardwalk offers stunning views of surfers riding the barrels.
