Island Hopping: Daku, Guyam & Naked Island

Island hopping is one of Siargao’s most popular activities, typically covering three islands in a single day:

  • Daku Island – The largest of the three, known for its wide sandy beach and fresh seafood lunches served by locals.
  • Guyam Island – A tiny palm-fringed islet perfect for swimming and relaxing.
  • Naked Island – A minimalist sandbar with no vegetation, offering panoramic views of the turquoise sea.

Tours depart from General Luna, with options for group packages or private boats. Expect to pay ₱1,500–2,500 per person, including lunch. The experience captures the essence of Siargao’s coastline — turquoise waters, salt air, and sun-soaked beaches.
