Sugba Lagoon

Hidden amidst Siargao’s mangrove forests, Sugba Lagoon is a paradise of emerald waters surrounded by limestone cliffs. Located on **Caob Island**, it’s a 20-minute boat ride from Del Carmen and a popular day trip from General Luna.

Visitors can enjoy paddleboarding, kayaking, swimming, or leaping from the famous wooden diving board. The floating boathouse offers a peaceful retreat, while the lagoon’s calm waters make it ideal for families and casual adventurers.

Day trips cost around ₱1,500–2,000 per person, including boat transfers and entrance fees. The lagoon is best visited in the morning for calm waters and fewer crowds.
