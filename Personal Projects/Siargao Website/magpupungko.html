Magpupungko Rock Pools

Located in **Pilar**, about 45 minutes from General Luna, Magpupungko Rock Pools are natural tidal pools revealed only during low tide. The pools feature crystal-clear waters, limestone formations, and calm swimming areas sheltered from the ocean.

Visitors can explore underwater rock formations, small caves, and even cliff-jump from the massive rock in the main pool. Timing is crucial — the pools are only accessible during low tide, so check tide schedules before visiting.

The area also includes Magpupungko Beach, perfect for sunbathing and enjoying scenic coastal views.
