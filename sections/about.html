Biography

I specialize in backend development, with strong expertise in web applications that integrate databases. My skills include designing and planning database architectures to ensure efficiency and scalability. While frontend development (HTML and CSS) is handled through AI-assisted tools, I focus on building robust backend systems and data-driven solutions.

Beyond software, I am also a computer repair technician. I perform hardware maintenance such as cleaning laptops and system units, diagnosing issues, replacing faulty components, installing operating systems (Linux and Windows), reformatting, and ensuring proper driver installation. In addition, I handle basic phone hardware repairs, including LCD replacement, button flex replacement, and battery replacement.

Outside of coding and repair work, I enjoy watching cybersecurity documentaries and technical videos. These cover topics such as the spread of malware like WannaCry, strategies for protecting against hackers and malicious actors, and tutorials on computer and phone repair. This continuous learning fuels both my technical growth and my passion for technology.
