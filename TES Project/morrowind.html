The Elder Scrolls III: Morrowind is the third game in the Elder Scrolls series, released in 2002. The story follows the player, a prisoner sent to the island of Vvardenfell in the province of Morrowind under mysterious circumstances.

The player is revealed to be the prophesied Nerevarine, the reincarnation of the ancient Dunmer hero Indoril Nerevar. Tasked with fulfilling the prophecy, the player must navigate the political and religious conflicts of Morrowind, gaining the trust of the Great Houses and Ashlander tribes.

The main antagonist, Dagoth Ur, resides within the volcanic Red Mountain, seeking to use the power of the Heart of Lorkhan to become a god and free Morrowind from Imperial rule. The player must gather Kagrenac’s tools, infiltrate Red Mountain, and destroy the Heart to defeat Dagoth Ur and fulfill their destiny.
